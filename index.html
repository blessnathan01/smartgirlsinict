The International Girls in ICT Day will be celebrated on 28 April 2022

International Girls in ICT will be celebrated the 11th Anniversary this year. In this regard, ITU invites stakeholders to contribute to the celebration by organizing programs, trainings, and activites during the year.

In Tanzania, SmartGirls In ICT programme will involving ICT trainings in twelve (12) secondary schools. This programme will be conducted for ten (10) weeks from third week of February 2022 to fourth week of April 2022.
